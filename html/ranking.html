How Points works ?
Points are calculated with the player match performance. Each matches played during the pool are calculated and added into player points. The points are calculted this way :
  • 25 Points if his team win or 10 points if it's a tie
  • 2 Points per Frags and 1 Points per Assist
  • Bonus are added if Kill/Death Delta is greater than 0, each positive difference of kill versus death give 3 Points
